# OutstandIQ — Debt Recovery Services Agreement

**IMPORTANT:** This is a template. Have a UK solicitor review it before first use. It is drafted for B2B commercial debt only — do not use for consumer debt recovery.

---

**This Agreement** is entered into on [DATE]

**Between:**

**OutstandIQ Ltd**, a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. [REGISTRATION NUMBER]), whose registered office is at [REGISTERED ADDRESS] ("OutstandIQ")

and

**[CLIENT COMPANY NAME]**, a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. [CLIENT COMPANY NUMBER]), whose registered address is at [CLIENT ADDRESS] ("the Client")

(together "the Parties")

---

## 1. SERVICES

1.1 OutstandIQ agrees to provide debt recovery services to the Client in respect of the outstanding invoices listed in Schedule 1 attached to this Agreement (the "Debts"), and such further invoices as the Client may instruct OutstandIQ to pursue from time to time in writing (including by email).

1.2 The services include: automated and manual communication with debtors via email, SMS, and telephone; negotiation of payment plans; and escalation recommendations. OutstandIQ does not provide legal services and will not commence court proceedings on the Client's behalf. Where legal action is recommended, OutstandIQ will notify the Client and the Client will instruct its own solicitors.

1.3 OutstandIQ will use reasonable skill and care in the provision of the services. OutstandIQ does not guarantee recovery of any specific amount or percentage of the Debts.

---

## 2. FEES

2.1 OutstandIQ's fee is a percentage of each amount successfully recovered and paid to the Client (the "Success Fee"). The applicable rate is set out in Schedule 1.

2.2 No fee is payable unless and until funds are received by the Client from the relevant debtor. A debt is "recovered" for the purposes of this Agreement when cleared funds are received into the Client's bank account.

2.3 Where a debtor makes payment in instalments, the Success Fee applies to each instalment as and when received.

2.4 There are no setup fees, case loading fees, administration charges, or membership fees under this Agreement.

2.5 OutstandIQ will invoice the Client monthly in arrears for all recoveries made during that calendar month. Payment is due within 14 days of invoice.

2.6 If the Client settles a Debt directly with a debtor during the term of this Agreement without notifying OutstandIQ, the Success Fee is payable as if OutstandIQ had recovered the full amount.

---

## 3. CLIENT OBLIGATIONS

3.1 The Client will provide OutstandIQ with accurate and complete information about each Debt, including original invoice copies, any correspondence with the debtor, and any dispute or credit note history.

3.2 The Client will promptly notify OutstandIQ of any direct contact received from a debtor, any payment received directly, any dispute raised, or any insolvency event affecting a debtor.

3.3 The Client will not contact debtors in respect of the Debts while OutstandIQ is actively pursuing them, except where agreed in advance with OutstandIQ.

3.4 The Client warrants that each Debt is a genuine, undisputed, legally enforceable obligation of the relevant debtor, that the Client has the right to assign recovery of the Debt to a third party, and that providing the Debt information to OutstandIQ does not breach any confidentiality obligation owed to the debtor.

---

## 4. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 This Agreement commences on the date it is signed by both Parties and continues until terminated.

4.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement on 30 days' written notice to the other Party.

4.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately on written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement and (where the breach is capable of remedy) fails to remedy it within 14 days of written notice requiring it to do so.

4.4 On termination, OutstandIQ will provide a final recovery report. The Success Fee remains payable on any amounts recovered during the notice period and, for a period of 60 days following termination, on any amounts recovered that result directly from OutstandIQ's prior outreach activity.

---

## 5. DATA PROTECTION

5.1 For the purposes of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018, the Client is the Data Controller and OutstandIQ is the Data Processor in respect of personal data relating to individual debtors.

5.2 OutstandIQ will process personal data only on the documented instructions of the Client (being the performance of the services under this Agreement), and for no other purpose.

5.3 OutstandIQ will ensure that all persons authorised to process the personal data are subject to appropriate confidentiality obligations.

5.4 OutstandIQ will implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction or damage.

5.5 OutstandIQ will assist the Client in responding to data subject access requests and in complying with its obligations under UK GDPR.

5.6 OutstandIQ will delete or return all personal data to the Client on termination of this Agreement, unless required by law to retain it.

5.7 OutstandIQ will notify the Client without undue delay on becoming aware of a personal data breach.

5.8 OutstandIQ is registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO Registration No. [ICO REGISTRATION NUMBER]).

---

## 6. CONFIDENTIALITY

6.1 Each Party agrees to keep confidential all information it receives from the other Party in connection with this Agreement that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would consider to be confidential, including information about debtors, business operations, and financial information.

6.2 This obligation does not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available other than through breach of this clause; (b) was already known to the receiving Party before disclosure; or (c) is required to be disclosed by law or regulatory authority.

6.3 This clause survives termination of the Agreement.

---

## 7. LIABILITY

7.1 Nothing in this Agreement limits or excludes either Party's liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation, or any other liability that cannot be limited or excluded by law.

7.2 Subject to clause 7.1, OutstandIQ's total liability to the Client under or in connection with this Agreement, whether in contract, tort (including negligence) or otherwise, shall not exceed the total Success Fees paid by the Client to OutstandIQ in the 12 months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

7.3 OutstandIQ shall not be liable for any indirect, consequential, or special loss, including loss of profits, loss of business, or loss of data.

---

## 8. GENERAL

8.1 **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties relating to its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, and agreements.

8.2 **Variation.** No variation of this Agreement is effective unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

8.3 **Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by and construed in accordance with the law of England and Wales. Each Party irrevocably submits to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

8.4 **Notices.** Notices under this Agreement may be given by email to the addresses specified in Schedule 1. Email notices are deemed received on the next working day after sending.

8.5 **Assignment.** The Client may not assign or transfer its rights or obligations under this Agreement without OutstandIQ's prior written consent. OutstandIQ may subcontract the performance of the services provided it remains responsible for their performance.
